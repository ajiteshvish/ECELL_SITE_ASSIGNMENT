About Us

E-Cell LNCT is the entrepreneurship cell of Lakshmi Narain College of Technology, Bhopal. We aim to foster the entrepreneurial spirit among students and provide a platform for budding entrepreneurs to transform their innovative ideas into successful ventures.

Our mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem that nurtures innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship through various initiatives, workshops, and events.
